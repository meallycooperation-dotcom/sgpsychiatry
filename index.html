Adult Psychiatry & Wellness

Compassionate, evidence-based mental health care for adults. In-person & telepsychiatry.

Patient Portal (253) 878-9211

Dr. Sammy Gathiru, DNP is a Board-Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner who provides a comprehensive approach to mental health treatment by incorporating psychopharmacology, psychotherapy, and alternative treatment modalities to best address your concern. Providing a client-centered method of treatment, which includes educating clients about their diagnosis, treatment options, and working with clients to develop a unique treatment plan tailored to fit each individual.

Psychiatric Services
Personalized care – therapy, medication management, and telepsychiatry.

Treatment: Our team is invested in helping you achieve your mental wellness goals. We treat psychiatric disorders not limited to: Mood, Anxiety, Depression, Bipolar, and Trauma-related disorders. Utilizing psychodynamic therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy, as well as other treatment modalities. We are trained and licensed to treat clients of all ages.

Consulting

For Psychiatric Services, Anxiety, Depression, Bipolar Disorder, Schizophrenia and more. Telepsychiatry /Telemedicine Appointments Also Available!

Financial

We accept most major insurance plans. However, due to the constant changes within the insurance industry, we advise checking with your insurance company for the latest provider information. We accept, cash, credit cards, and checks. This information is subject to change without notice.

Process

We do an initial complete psychological evaluation on all clients. Including use of effective screening tools to systematically and thoroughly give an indicator of psychiatric diagnosis
